Welcome to Snake Snack!
Silvers the Snake thank you for coming, he is starting to become quite hungry. However, he isn't the best at choosing his food, he needs to stay away from the unhealty food, to not get sick. Will you help Silvers to get the healty food so he can grow and not get sick?

I am sure you already know this, but lets just go over it one more time for the people in the back!

To control Silvers the Snake on the screen you have to push the arrow keys. Make sure you keep away from hitting the border or yourself it will make you go game over. Make him grow and earn points for as long as you can. Let's see how far you can go!

Healty Food
Every time you eat some of the healty food you grow both in length and in points.

Unhealty Food
Keep silvers away from all the unhealty food it might let him grow, but your points will fall
